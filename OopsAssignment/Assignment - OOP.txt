Write a program that:

1. Collects a family's details from the console. This can include:

	a. Firstname
	b. Lastname
	c. Age
	d. Phone Number
		1. Office
		2. Home
		3. Mobile
	e. Address
		1. Permanent
		2. Current
	f. Position (eg: Father, Daughter etc.)

Store it as a complex data structure and then display all the details of the family.

2. Search on the basis of any parameter (Firstname, Lastname, Phone Number) and display all the details of the 'matching' family member.

3. Using the search logic above, update any parameter of a family member and display all of his / her information.

4. Sort the family members on the basis of any parameter and display all of them in order.
